🔥 Revenue is not cash

Growth can still punch a hole in your bank account.

Ecommerce operators do not usually die from lack of sales. They die from cash timing, inventory commitments, ad spend addiction, and payout delays wearing a fake mustache called “scale.”

📦

Inventory Trap

Restock too late and you stock out. Restock too early and your cash gets locked in boxes sitting on a shelf.

📣

Ad Spend Burn

ROAS can look fine while cash disappears because spend leaves today and profit limps in later.

⏳

Payout Delay

Shopify, Amazon, TikTok, Stripe, and PayPal do not care that your bills are due before deposits land.

🧾

Debt Drag

Daily, weekly, and monthly payments quietly turn healthy revenue into a treadmill with a payment processor.

⚙️ How it works

Five steps from “where did the money go?” to “here’s the move.”

Start with rough numbers, improve the forecast with files, then use the output to make better operating decisions before the cash crunch gets teeth.

1

Intake

Enter cash, revenue, margin, ads, inventory plans, debt payments, payout timing, and desired buffer.

2

Upload

Add Shopify, Amazon, TikTok, Etsy, Stripe, PayPal, P&L, bank, ad, or inventory files when available.

3

Forecast

Generate conservative, base, and optimistic 90-day scenarios with cash low points and pressure alerts.

4

Decide

Compare options: cut spend, delay purchases, restock sooner, preserve cash, or prepare capital.

5

Act

Use the action plan, planner, prompts, and memo to make the next move without guessing in the fog.
