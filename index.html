🔥 Revenue is not cash

Growth can still punch a hole in your bank account.

Ecommerce operators do not usually die from lack of sales. They die from cash timing, inventory commitments, ad spend addiction, and payout delays wearing a fake mustache called “scale.”

📦

Inventory Trap

Restock too late and you stock out. Restock too early and cash gets locked in boxes.

📣

Ad Spend Burn

ROAS can look fine while cash disappears because spend leaves today and profit limps in later.

⏳

Payout Delay

Platforms do not care that bills are due before deposits land.

🧾

Debt Drag

Daily, weekly, and monthly payments quietly turn healthy revenue into treadmill money.
