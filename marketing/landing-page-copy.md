## FAQ Section

### What is the 90-Day Ecommerce Cash Flow Planner?

It is a spreadsheet template that helps ecommerce sellers forecast cash inflows, cash outflows, inventory purchases, ad spend, recurring payments, runway, and possible working capital planning gaps over the next 13 weeks.

### Who should use it?

Shopify sellers, Amazon sellers, Etsy sellers, TikTok Shop sellers, WooCommerce stores, DTC brands, and marketplace sellers that deal with inventory, ads, platform payouts, and working capital pressure.

### Is this accounting software?

No. It is a cash flow planning workbook. It helps you forecast timing risk and operating cash pressure. It does not replace accounting software, bookkeeping, tax support, or professional advice.

### Does this determine approval for capital?

No. It does not determine approval, qualification, eligibility, or guarantee outcomes. It helps estimate whether your forecast shows a possible working capital timing gap.

### Can I use this with ChatGPT?

Yes. The planner includes AI prompts you can use to ask ChatGPT for weekly reviews, scenario planning, gap diagnosis, inventory recommendations, and cash preservation plans.

### Is this only for Shopify?

No. It works for Shopify, Amazon, Etsy, TikTok Shop, WooCommerce, Walmart Marketplace, and multi-channel ecommerce sellers.
