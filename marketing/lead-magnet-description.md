The 90-Day Ecommerce Cash Flow Planner helps Shopify, Amazon, Etsy, TikTok Shop, WooCommerce, and marketplace sellers forecast the next 13 weeks of cash flow so they can avoid preventable cash crunches.

## Plain-English Description

Sales can be up while cash is still getting body-slammed by inventory, ads, platform fees, payroll, debt payments, shipping, taxes, and payout delays.

The 90-Day Ecommerce Cash Flow Planner gives sellers a simple but powerful spreadsheet to see what cash is coming in, what cash is going out, when inventory purchases hit, whether ad spend is creating pressure, and when a working capital gap may appear.

It is built for ecommerce operators who do not want to become finance nerds, but also do not want to wake up one morning wondering why revenue is growing and the bank account looks like it got jumped in an alley.

## Primary Pain Point

Most ecommerce sellers track sales.

Fewer track cash timing.

That is where the ugly stuff hides.

A seller can have strong revenue, solid demand, and growing orders — then still run short because inventory, ads, debt payments, payroll, supplier invoices, refunds, and platform payout delays all hit before cash catches up.

## What the Planner Helps Sellers Answer

- How much cash will I have over the next 90 days?
- Which week does cash get tight?
- Will my next inventory order create a cash gap?
- Can my cash position support my ad spend plan?
- Are debt payments reducing my operating flexibility?
- Am I profitable but still cash-constrained?
- How much working capital might I need to stay above my safe buffer?
- What expenses, orders, or campaigns should I adjust first?
- What data should I confirm before making a major spending decision?

## Core Benefits

### 1. Forecast Cash Before It Gets Tight

See projected weekly beginning cash, cash in, cash out, net cash flow, ending cash, and days of runway.

### 2. Spot Inventory Cash Traps

Plan restocks by SKU, supplier lead time, purchase cost, reorder point, and cash impact.

### 3. Control Ad Spend Pressure

Compare weekly ad spend against revenue, ROAS, inventory availability, and cash runway.

### 4. Track Debt and Financing Payments

Map recurring payments so loans, advances, credit cards, and platform financing do not quietly strangle operating cash.

### 5. Identify Working Capital Gaps Early

Estimate when cash may fall below a minimum safe buffer and calculate a planning range for possible working capital needs.

### 6. Use AI to Review the Forecast Weekly

Includes ready-to-use ChatGPT prompts for weekly analysis, funding gap diagnosis, inventory restock review, ad spend review, and cash preservation planning.
