# Video Content Plan

## Purpose

This file outlines a video content plan for promoting **The 90-Day Ecommerce Cash Flow Planner** and the **Ecommerce Cash Flow Forecaster GPT**.

The goal is to create useful, high-trust content for ecommerce sellers while driving downloads, GPT usage, consultations, or funding-readiness conversations.

## Core Video Positioning

The recurring message:

**Revenue is not cash.**

Most ecommerce sellers do not fail because they cannot generate sales.

They get squeezed because inventory, ads, debt payments, shipping, refunds, and payout delays hit before cash catches up.

## Primary Content Pillars

1. Cash flow truth-telling
2. Inventory cash traps
3. Ad spend and ROAS myths
4. Platform payout delays
5. Debt-service and payment stacking
6. Working capital gap education
7. Weekly forecast reviews
8. AI-powered finance workflows

## Target Platforms

- YouTube long-form
- YouTube Shorts
- TikTok
- Instagram Reels

- Facebook groups

- Email newsletter embeds

**Hook:**  
ROAS is not cash.

**Hook:**  
The sale happened today. The cash did not.

### Short 6: Working Capital Gap

A funding gap is not a panic button. It is a planning number.

**Hook:**
