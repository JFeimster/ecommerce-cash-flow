Formula engine

The boring math that prevents exciting emergencies.

These are the core formulas behind the planner. They are not fancy. That is the point.

Core cash equationBeginning Cash + Cash Inflows - Cash Outflows = Ending Cash

Ending cash from one week becomes beginning cash for the next week.

Funding Gap=MAX(0, Minimum Cash Threshold - Lowest Ending Cash)

Shows how far the forecast dips below the seller’s safe cash buffer.

Suggested Planning Amount=Funding Gap × (1 + Cushion %)

Adds a cushion for messy data, timing risk, and ugly little ecommerce surprises.

Days Cash Runway=Ending Cash / Average Daily Cash Out

Turns cash balance into time, because bills care about dates.

Cash Flow StatusCritical if cash < 0 Red if cash < threshold Yellow if runway < target Watch if net cash flow < 0 OK otherwise

Converts spreadsheet rows into a plain-English operating signal.

Inventory Purchases by Week=SUMIF(Planned Order Week, Current Week, Purchase Cost)

Pulls purchase orders into the exact week they hit cash.
